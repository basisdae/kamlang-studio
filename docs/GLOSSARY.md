# KL Builder — Glossary

Domain terms used across product, design, and engineering.

For product vision, see `docs/PRODUCT.md`.
For recorded decisions, see `docs/DECISIONS.md`.
For engineering rules, see `AGENTS.md`.

---

## Food Cost

**Definition**

Raw ingredient cost only.

---

## Suggested Price

Selling price generated by GP settings.

---

## Builder

Recipe creation workflow.

---

## Recipe

A standard kitchen recipe (สูตรมาตรฐาน) — ingredients, yield, and steps.

Selling price belongs to **Menu**, not Recipe.

See `app/recipes/README.md`.

---

## Menu

A sellable product derived from a Standard Recipe.

Carries its own selling price, category, and optional packaging.

See `app/menu/README.md`.

Examples: กะเพราหมูสับราดข้าว, กะเพราหมูสับ + ไข่ดาว, เบอร์เกอร์หมู.

---

## Builder Draft

An experimental recipe saved from the Builder workspace (`SavedRecipeRepository`).

Not the same as a Standard Recipe.

---

## Ingredient

A purchasable item.
